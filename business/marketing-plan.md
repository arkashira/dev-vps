# Marketing Plan
## Positioning
### Primary Positioning
Managed VPS for developers, where setup, security, and scalability are handled so you can focus on coding.

### Alternative Positioning Options
1. **DevOps in a Box**: A hassle-free, all-in-one VPS solution for developers, covering setup, security, and scalability needs.
2. **Code, Not Config**: A managed VPS service that lets developers focus on coding, while we handle the infrastructure and security.
3. **Development Simplified**: A streamlined VPS solution for developers, eliminating the need for manual setup, security, and scalability management.
