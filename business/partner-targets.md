# Partner Targets
## Introduction
To enhance the value proposition of dev-vps, we will integrate with key SaaS and API partners, focusing on those that offer affiliate or revenue-share programs. This will not only expand our offerings but also provide additional revenue streams.

## Partner Integration Roadmap
The following partners have been selected based on their relevance to developers, the potential for integration, and the presence of affiliate or revenue-share programs.

1. **DigitalOcean**
	* Free-tier limits: $100 credit for new users
	* Integration effort: M
	* Value-add: Simplifies infrastructure management for developers
	* Affiliate program: Yes, offering a $25 credit for each referred user
2. **GitHub**
	* Free-tier limits: Unlimited public repositories, 500 MB of storage, and 1 collaborator for private repositories
	* Integration effort: S
	* Value-add: Streamlines development workflows and version control
	* Affiliate program: No, but offers a GitHub Developer Program with benefits for developers
3. **AWS Lambda**
	* Free-tier limits: 1 million free requests per month
	* Integration effort: L
	* Value-add: Enables serverless computing and scalability for developers
	* Affiliate program: Yes, through the AWS Affiliate Program
4. **Datadog**
	* Free-tier limits: 1 host, 1 database, and 1 service for monitoring
	* Integration effort: M
	* Value-add: Provides monitoring and analytics for application performance
	* Affiliate program: Yes, offering a $10 credit for each referred user
5. **SendGrid**
	* Free-tier limits: 100 emails per day
	* Integration effort: S
	* Value-add: Simplifies email delivery and management for developers
	* Affiliate program: Yes, offering a $10 credit for each referred user
6. **CircleCI**
	* Free-tier limits: 1 user, 1 project, and 1,000 credits per month
	* Integration effort: M
	* Value-add: Automates continuous integration and delivery for developers
	* Affiliate program: No, but offers a CircleCI Partner Program with benefits for partners
7. **New Relic**
	* Free-tier limits: 1 user, 1 application, and 1,000,000 events per day
	* Integration effort: M
	* Value-add: Provides application performance monitoring and analytics
	* Affiliate program: Yes, offering a $10 credit for each referred user

## Prioritization
The partners have been prioritized based on the following criteria:

1. Relevance to the dev-vps target audience
2. Presence of affiliate or revenue-share programs
3. Integration effort required
4. Value-add to the dev-vps platform

By integrating with these partners, dev-vps can enhance its offerings, provide additional value to developers, and generate additional revenue streams through affiliate and revenue-share programs.
