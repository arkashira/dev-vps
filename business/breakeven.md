- Compute: $0.05/user/mo (based on 1 vCPU, 2GB RAM)
- Storage: $0.03/user/mo (10GB SSD)
- Bandwidth: $0.02/user/mo (1TB/mo)
- **Total cost per active user: $0.10/user/mo**

1. **Basic**: $5/mo

- **High end**: $50/user (targeted outreach)

- Average customer lifespan: 12 months
- Average revenue per user (ARPU): $20/mo (weighted average)
- **LTV**: $240/user

## Break-even users count
- **Fixed costs**: $5,000/mo (salaries, overhead, etc.)
- **Variable costs**: $0.10/user/mo
- **Break-even users**: 5,000 users (assuming 50% margin)

- **Basic tier**: 1,000 users × $5/mo = $5,000 MRR
